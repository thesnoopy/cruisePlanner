# Privacy Policy for Cruise Planner

Cruise Planner is designed for private cruise planning on iPhone.

## Data Processing

Cruise Planner stores the data you enter in the app, such as cruise details, itinerary items, excursions, travel bookings, and linked documents.

This data is stored locally on your device.

## Optional WebDAV Sync

If you choose to configure WebDAV sync, the app transfers your app data and documents to the WebDAV server you specify. This feature is optional and only used when you enable and configure it yourself.

## Personal Data

Cruise Planner does not require account creation.

The app does not provide a built-in public social platform or user profile system.
